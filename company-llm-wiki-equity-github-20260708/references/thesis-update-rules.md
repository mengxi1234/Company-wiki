# Thesis Update Rules

## Company Thesis vs Security Thesis

Separate the two:

- Company thesis: business quality, durability, moat, management, financial quality.
- Security thesis: current price, expectations, upside/downside, margin of safety.

A company thesis can strengthen while the security thesis remains `not decision-grade` because market price, consensus, and reverse DCF are missing.

## Hypothesis Status

Use statuses such as:

- `增强`
- `削弱`
- `保持待验证`
- `新增待验证`
- `破坏`

Every hypothesis needs:

- Supporting evidence
- Opposing evidence or risk
- Observable falsifier
- Current status

## Risk Rules

For each risk, include:

- Risk name
- Level: `高 / 中高 / 中 / 低`
- Evidence source
- Inference
- Tracking metric
- Source ID(s)

News can raise a risk flag, but it should not prove the risk without filings, official disclosures, transcripts, or primary-source evidence.

## Valuation Rules

Translate narrative into variables:

- Revenue growth
- Segment mix
- Gross margin
- Operating margin
- FCF margin
- Capex and supply-chain commitments
- Working capital
- Terminal competitive structure
- Discount rate or multiples
- Scenario probability

Mark each variable as sourced from filings, transcripts, broker assumptions, industry reports, or still `待验证`.

## Decision Log

Append one row for each material research update:

- Date
- Research action
- Original judgment
- New judgment
- Reason for change
- Source IDs
- Credibility
- Verification gaps
- Impacted pages

Do not overwrite old investment views without recording why they changed.

## Final Report

For substantial updates, report:

1. Sources successfully ingested
2. Sources inaccessible or requiring manual upload
3. Files updated or created
4. New key facts
5. New inferences
6. Strengthened assumptions
7. Weakened assumptions
8. Most important disagreements
9. Most important tracking variables
10. Next research steps
